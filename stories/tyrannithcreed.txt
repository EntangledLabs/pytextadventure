These are the terms of the Tyrannith Creed.
We, the signers, agree to abide by
the following Articles, to which no exception
will be made unless under extreme need and
with the express agreement of the other
signers.

Article I
The Tyrannith shall not betray his/her fellow
Tyrannith.

Article II
The Tyrannith shall not be the enemy of a fellow
Tyrannith.

Article III
The Tyrannith shall assist a fellow Tyrannith
in dire need.

Article IV
The Tyrannith shall not lie to a fellow Tyrannith.

Article V
The Creed of the Tyrannith shall not be revealed
to anyone who is not a Tyrannith.
